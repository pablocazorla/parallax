Caz√∫ Parallax

A simple parallax plugin of jQuery, for "parallax" effect.

Download as zip file
View in Github

How to use

1 Add jquery.cazu.parallax.js to your page:

<script type="text/javascript" src="js/jquery.cazu.parallax.js"></script>

2 In your section or div to "parallax", just add a <div class="bg"/> with the image/video/slider you want:

<section class="element-to-parallax">
  <div class="bg">
    <img src="img/image-example.jpg"/>
  </div>
  ...

  Your content...

</section>

3 In your script, just add a call to the plugin:

$('document').ready(function(){
  $('.element-to-parallax').parallax();
});

Use with video background

<section class="element-to-parallax">
  <div class="bg">
    <video src="video.mp4" autoplay loop></video>
  </div>
  ...

  Your content...

</section>

Use also with a background image or a carousel of images
